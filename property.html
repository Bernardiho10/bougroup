Who Can Benefit from PMS?

We Are Here to Grow and Organise Your Real Estate Portfolio

Landlords

Manage properties, view tenant info, and track rental revenue effortlessly.

Property Managers

Oversee maintenance, share vacant units with agents, and handle tenant applications.

Tenants

Pay rent, report maintenance issues, and manage lease agreements seamlessly.

Agents

Agents will collaborate indirectly with the PMS through communication with property managers or landlords, who will be responsible for entering all relevant data related to the agents' activities, such as property inspections and tenant viewings.

This streamlined process ensures that all necessary information is accurately recorded and managed within the PMS, enhancing efficiency and coordination between all parties involved.

Tenant Acquisition Flow

Share Vacant Units

Property managers share vacant units with agents to find prospective tenants.

Agent Marketing

Agents market the available units to potential tenants.

Schedule Inspections

Agents request inspection dates from property managers, which are then communicated to tenants.

Feedback Collection

Property managers collect inspection feedback and start the tenant application process.

Landlord Approval

Applications are reviewed by the landlord, who makes final decisions.

Tenant Account Setup

Upon approval, a tenant account is created, and details are sent via SMS.
